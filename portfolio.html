Minesweeper

In my senior year of high school, I was a part of a program called the Independent Study & Mentorship program, otherwise known as "ISM". In ISM, I found a candidate for a mentor through cold contacting and interviewing and that mentor is an engineer for game development. Because of that, I was inspired to work in Unity (C#) to code my own version of minesweeper (which I was hooked on playing at the time). On screen is the hard mode, but I had easy and medium mode implemented as well. I then exported the project using WebGL so that others could play my version of minesweeper online.
